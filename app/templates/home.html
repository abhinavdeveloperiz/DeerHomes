About Us

deerhomes® is a team of highly skilled professionals including Architects, Interior designers and Engineers sharing a deep passion for excellence, blending creativity with technical precision. We believe we can make life better for our clients through design. A well-designed space enhances the quality of everyday living. Be it a home or a commercial space, circulation patterns, and creation of a comfortable micro environment is the result of careful planning, creative thinking, and flawless execution. By creating sustainable building modules using materials with a reduced carbon footprint, we try to create a better building system for the future generations. Top-notch design and planning ensures that our projects create a better quality of life for our clients while efficiency in design is translated into cost savings in all the major projects of deerhomes. Our versatile design philosophy encompasses various architectural styles of the past to present so our portfolio ranges from a contemporary minimalistic design approach to classical renaissance styles and traditional Kerala architecture styles. Our project management team led by experienced project managers, engineers and contractors ensure that the concepts and ideas originating from the drawing table are converted into built space flawlessly within the scheduled time. Deerhomes was founded in 1999 by Deepak Somarajan, an architectural engineer from NIT Calicut, and his wife Parvathy Vijayan, a structural engineer from TKM Engineering College, who later joined the company as its director.
