About Us

deerhomes® is a team of highly skilled professionals including Architects, Interior designers, and Engineers sharing a deep passion for excellence, blending creativity with technical precision. We believe we can make life better for our clients through design. A well-designed space enhances the quality of everyday living.

By creating sustainable building modules using materials with a reduced carbon footprint, we aim to create better building systems for future generations. Our versatile design philosophy ranges from contemporary minimalistic designs to classical and traditional styles. Top-notch planning ensures efficiency and cost-effectiveness in all major projects.
